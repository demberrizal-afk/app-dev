# My Favorite Character

## Deku from *My Hero Academia*

**Deku**, whose real name is *Izuku Midoriya*, is my favorite anime character.  
He is known for his **determination**, **kind heart**, and **never-give-up attitude**.

---

### Reasons Why I Like Deku:
1. He always strives to become better.
2. He cares deeply about his friends.
3. His hero journey is inspiring.
